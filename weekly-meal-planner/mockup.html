Monday Mediterranean Diet
Herb-Crusted Baked Cod
Flaky cod baked with lemon, garlic, and fresh herbs over a bed of cherry tomatoes and spinach.
PREP10 mins
COOK20 mins
SERVES4
Ingredients
4 cod fillets (6 oz each)
3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 lemons, zested and juiced
1 cup cherry tomatoes
Instructions
1
Preheat oven to 400°F. Whisk olive oil, lemon zest, garlic, and herbs.
2
Coat cod fillets, place on tomatoes, bake 18–20 mins until flaky.
