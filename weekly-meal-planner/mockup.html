Monday Mediterranean Diet
Greek Lemon Herb Salmon
Pan-seared salmon with a bright lemon-herb marinade, roasted cherry tomatoes, and wilted spinach.
PREP 10 mins
COOK 20 mins
SERVES 4
Ingredients
4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 lemons, zested and juiced
1 cup cherry tomatoes
Instructions
1
Whisk olive oil, lemon zest, garlic, and oregano into a marinade.
2
Coat salmon and sear skin-side down for 4 minutes.
