Scan your groceries. Get a week of dinners. No missing ingredients, no extra trips.
Tired of staring at your pantry wondering what to make for dinner? Or buying groceries and still ending up lost at 6pm? Weekly Meal Planner scans your grocery receipt and builds a full week of personalized recipes from what you already have — no extra store runs, no realizing halfway through a recipe that you're missing something. How it works: → Photograph your grocery receipt or scan items directly with your camera → We extract your ingredients automatically → Get 7 complete recipes built around what's in your pantry right now Features: • Receipt scanning powered by AI — pulls every item in seconds • Barcode scanner to add pantry staples one product at a time • 7 personalized daily recipes, Monday through Sunday • Save individual recipes or full weekly menus to revisit anytime • Beautifully formatted recipe cards you can share with family • Mediterranean diet guidelines backed by the Mayo Clinic • Your current meal plan stays accessible for 7 days Built for the person standing in front of their cupboards — not for people who already know what they're cooking.
