Privacy Policy

Weekly Meal Planner  ·  Effective date: June 22, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 22, 2026

The short version: Your pantry and meal plan data stays on your device. We never sell your data. The only time anything leaves your phone is when a receipt photo or ingredient list is sent to an AI service to generate recipes — and that is not stored.

1. Who We Are

Weekly Meal Planner is an independent mobile application developed by mmccanse. If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at mmccanse@yahoo.com.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

We collect only what is necessary to make the app work. Here is everything, with no omissions:

Receipt photos
When you photograph or upload a grocery receipt, that image is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI service to extract ingredient names. The image is transmitted securely and is not stored on any server controlled by us. Anthropic's handling of this data is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Pantry items
The ingredients you add to your pantry are stored locally on your device using your phone's built-in secure storage (AsyncStorage). They are never uploaded to any server.

Meal plans and saved recipes
Generated meal plans, saved recipes, and saved menus are all stored locally on your device. They do not leave your phone.

Ingredient list sent to AI
When you generate a meal plan, your combined pantry and receipt ingredients are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate recipes. No personal identifying information is included. This list is not stored by us.

Barcode scans
When you scan a product barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts, a free open-source food database, to look up the product name. No identifying information is attached to this request.

Food photography
Recipe and ingredient photos are fetched from Unsplash using a search term (e.g., "salmon mediterranean food"). These are standard API requests with no personal data attached. Photo URLs are cached locally on your device.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration or login — we do not know who you are
  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No cookies (mobile app)
  • No data sold or shared with advertisers, ever

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Anthropic Claude API — AI processing for receipt scanning and recipe generation. Privacy Policy
  • Unsplash — Food photography. Privacy Policy
  • Open Food Facts — Barcode product lookup. Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

All user-generated data (pantry items, meal plans, saved recipes) is stored exclusively on your device using your operating system's standard application storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Because all your data is stored on your own device, you have complete control over it at all times. You can:

  • View all stored data within the app (pantry, saved recipes, saved menus)
  • Delete individual items at any time within the app
  • Delete all data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app

If you are in the European Union or California and have additional rights questions, please contact us directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
