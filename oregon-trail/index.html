Independence, Missouri — 1848

You are about to set off on the trail to Oregon in a covered wagon with a party of five. You will face rivers, storms, broken axles, thieves, and disease. Do you have what it takes to reach the Willamette Valley?

A wealthy relative has left you an unlimited fortune. Money will never be a problem — everything else in the West still can be.
